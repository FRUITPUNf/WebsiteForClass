About Our Company

At P3 RF Engineering, we’re more than just a workplace — we’re a team of innovators committed to shaping the future of RF technology. From advanced communication systems to cutting-edge defense solutions, our engineers are empowered to lead with curiosity and impact.

With headquarters across the southeastern U.S., we provide engineering services and field operations for both public and private clients. Our work culture fosters collaboration, continuous learning, and respect for diverse perspectives. Whether you're building prototypes, testing in the field, or managing client relationships — you’ll play a key role in delivering excellence.

Why Join P3?

  • Innovative RF, telecom, and defense projects
  • Supportive, team-first culture
  • Career growth through mentorship & hands-on experience
  • Flexible work options & competitive benefits

What You’ll Work On

From lab-based RF simulations to real-world antenna testing, our projects span the entire engineering lifecycle. You’ll have opportunities to contribute to wireless system design, integration, testing, and deployment. No two days are the same, and every project brings new learning and innovation.
