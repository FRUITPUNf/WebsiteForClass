Locations

P3 RF Engineering proudly operates across the Southeastern United States. Our offices in South Carolina, North Carolina, and Georgia anchor a region where defense, telecommunications, and critical infrastructure intersect.

Each of our hubs serves a strategic purpose—providing expert RF engineering solutions from local offices with deep ties to their communities. Whether it’s a rapid field deployment in Atlanta or collaborative system testing near Charlotte, our engineers are embedded where innovation meets need.

Charleston, SC

Located near major ports and military bases, our Charleston office supports coastal infrastructure, defense, and aerospace initiatives.

Charlotte, NC

Charlotte serves as our regional engineering headquarters—housing system design teams and prototyping labs for commercial clients.

Atlanta, GA

Home to a growing telecom hub, our Atlanta branch focuses on wireless site audits, spectrum testing, and smart grid technologies.
